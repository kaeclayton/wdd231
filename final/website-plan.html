Kae Clayton WDD231 Final Project - Website Plan

Site Name:

"My Brick Collection"

mylegobrickcollection.com

Site Purpose:

Share my passion for LEGO with others with that same passion and hopefully ignite the love of LEGO in others. This will be achieved by having a page dedicated to a gallery of all my sets. A landing page will have LEGO info and exciting sets. The third page will be a form where they can share their favorite sets, or one for me to check out.

Scenarios:

One scenario may be another LEGO collector who is interested in other's collections in order to compare and share. Some questions asked by visitors may be: What is the longest it has ever taken you to build a set? What is/are your favorite set/sets? What is a good beginner set? Why do people like LEGO so much?

Color Schema:

headings: LEGO red - #B40000, deep LEGO blue - #003C88, and white text - #FFFFFF

paragraph text: black: #000000

background color: LEGO yellow - #F2CD377, and white - #FFFFFF (potentially)

accent color: LEGO green - #4B9F4A

Typography:

Arial Black for Headings, Arial Bold for paragraphs. Back up fonts: Impact, Verdana, Sans-serif
